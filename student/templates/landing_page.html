About the System

The SMS Portal is designed to bridge the gap between administration, faculty, and students. By digitizing manual processes, we aim to create a paperless, efficient, and transparent educational environment. Whether you are checking your GPA, submitting term grades, or managing the academic calendar, our system ensures accuracy and ease of access.
